Idle Cost Analysis

True Cost of Idling

The real financial impact of idle time per asset — fuel waste, accelerated depreciation, and service cost impact combined.

💡

How this is calculated: Idle fuel cost uses the same idle burn rate method as the Fuel Analyst page. Depreciation uses (current value − end of life value) ÷ remaining life per hour or km. Servicing is scheduled routine service cost per hour. Maintenance is estimated annual repair cost spread across expected annual usage. Set these per asset on the Assets page.
