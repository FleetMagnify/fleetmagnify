Idle Cost Analysis

True Cost of Idling

The real financial impact of idle time per asset — fuel waste, accelerated depreciation, and service cost impact combined.

💡

How this is calculated: Idle fuel cost uses the same idle burn rate method as the Fuel Analyst page. Depreciation cost is the asset's purchase price divided by its expected life hours, multiplied by idle hours. Service cost uses a blended service cost per hour (calculated by you from your own service history) multiplied by idle hours. Set or adjust these figures per asset on the Assets page.
