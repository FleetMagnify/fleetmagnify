Fuel Consumption Breakdown

True travel vs. idle fuel cost, calculated from your actual fuel invoices and telematics hours.

💡

How this is calculated: Total litres come directly from your fuel invoices, which is always accurate. Idle fuel is estimated using each asset's idle burn rate (litres/hour) multiplied by idle hours from telematics. Travel fuel is the remainder. Set or adjust the idle burn rate per asset on the Assets page.
