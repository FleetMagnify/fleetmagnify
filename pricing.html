Common questions
How does billing work?
You are billed monthly based on the number of active assets in your account. Add or remove assets at any time — billing adjusts the following month.
Is there a minimum commitment?
No. There are no minimum terms, no setup fees, and no lock-in periods. You pay month to month for exactly what you have.
What counts as a machine vs a truck?
Machinery includes excavators, dump trucks, wheel loaders, motor graders, bulldozers, cranes, forklifts, water trucks, and other plant assets that use OEM telematics. Trucks covers light vehicles, rigid trucks, and semi trailers operating on public roads using eRoad and BP Fleet Card.
Do I need to pay for integrations?
No. All integrations — eRoad, BP Fleet Card, VisionLink, Komtrax, ConSite, and all other OEM telematics platforms — are included in the base price. There are no per-integration fees.
Can I start with just trucks or just machinery?
Yes. Start with whatever assets you want to connect first. You can add additional asset types at any time — billing simply reflects what is active in your account.
When is Pro Insights available?
Pro Insights is currently in development. Pricing and availability will be announced to existing customers first. If you would like to be notified, contact us directly.
