Emissions Per Kilometre

Fill-to-fill CO₂ analysis for on-road vehicles using fuel consumption and eROAD odometer readings.

💡

How this is calculated: Each period runs from one fuel fill to the next. Litres come from the second fill; kilometres driven are the odometer difference between readings closest to each fill date. Total emissions = litres × emission factor for the vehicle's fuel type (Diesel 2.68, Petrol 2.31, Hybrid 1.5, Electric 0 kg CO₂/L).
