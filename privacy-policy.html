Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how [FleetMagnify Limited] ("FleetMagnify", "we", "us") collects, uses, stores, and discloses personal information in connection with the FleetMagnify platform (the "Service"), in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

1. What We Collect

Account information

When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, company name, and fleet size, as provided during registration.

Fleet and fuel data

To provide the Service, we collect data about your vehicles and machinery, including asset names, registrations, fuel purchase records, telematics readings (hours, idle time, distance travelled, odometer readings), and related operational data. This data is either uploaded by you via email (as CSV exports from your telematics or fuel provider platforms) or entered manually within the platform.

Where fuel or telematics data includes driver names (for example, where a fuel card transaction is recorded against a driver rather than a vehicle), this may constitute personal information about your employees or contractors. You are responsible for ensuring you have the appropriate authority to share this information with us, including notifying affected individuals where required.

Billing information

Payment card details are collected and processed directly by our payment processor, Stripe. We do not store your full card details on our own systems.

Usage data

We may collect information about how you use the Service, such as pages visited and features used, to help us improve the product.

We do not sell your personal information or Customer Data. We may disclose information to:

  • A buyer or successor, in the event of a sale, merger, or restructure of the business, subject to equivalent privacy protections.

We retain your Customer Data for as long as your account is active. We take reasonable steps to protect your information, including access controls that restrict each account's data to that account only (row-level security), encrypted data transmission, and secure authentication. No system can be guaranteed completely secure, and you should also take care to protect your own account credentials.

7. Your Rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to access the personal information we hold about you and to request correction of that information. You may also request deletion of your data as described in Section 5. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details in Section 8.

8. Contact and Complaints

For any privacy-related questions, access requests, correction requests, or deletion requests, contact us at support@fleetmagnify.com.

If you are not satisfied with how we have handled your personal information, you may lodge a complaint with the New Zealand Office of the Privacy Commissioner.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via email or in-app notice. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
