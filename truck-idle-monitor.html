Idle Hours & Fuel Cost Monitor

Track idle fuel cost and idle-hour trends per vehicle, or compare idling cost across your whole on-road fleet.

💡

How this is calculated: Idle litres for each fill-to-fill fuel period are idle hours × calibrated idle burn rate (L/hr). Idle fuel cost prices those litres at that period's own fuel rate; travel fuel cost is the remainder. The idle burn rate comes from the fuel regression engine when calibrated, or a default rate otherwise. Days are flagged as unusually high when idle hours exceed 3 hours — using daily telematics readings directly, not fuel-period buckets.
