Setup

Up and running in under 10 minutes.

Two things to set up: your API keys and your diesel price. After that, you never touch it again.

1
Add Your Assets
Download the Asset Import Template, complete the Machinery and Trucks sheets, and upload to add your entire fleet at once.
→ Download template from Upload Data page
2
Set Your Fuel Price
Enter your current bulk diesel price per litre in Settings. This is applied to all machinery fuel calculations from your OEM telematics data.
→ Settings → Machinery Fuel Cost per Litre
3
Connect Your APIs
Paste your eRoad API key, BP Fleet Card key, and OEM telematics credentials into the Integrations page. Data flows automatically from that night onwards.
→ Fleet Management → Integrations
