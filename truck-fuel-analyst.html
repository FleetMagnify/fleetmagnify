Fuel Cost Per Kilometre

Fill-to-fill cost analysis for on-road vehicles using BP fuel purchases and eROAD odometer readings.

💡

How this is calculated: Each period runs from one fuel fill to the next. Litres and cost come from the second fill; kilometres driven are the odometer difference between readings closest to each fill date. Periods above $0.155/km are flagged as above benchmark.
