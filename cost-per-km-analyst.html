Total Cost Per Kilometre

Blended fill-to-fill analysis for on-road vehicles — fuel, depreciation, and maintenance per km, with manual overrides per period.

💡

How this is calculated: Fuel cost per km comes from fill-to-fill periods (BP purchases and Navman odometer). Depreciation per km is (current value − end of life value) ÷ remaining km. Maintenance per km is annual repair cost ÷ estimated annual km. Use overrides when a calculated figure doesn't reflect reality for a specific period.
