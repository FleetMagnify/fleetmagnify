Utilisation Analysis

Asset Utilisation Performance

Compare actual operating hours against available capacity and your target utilisation benchmark — day by day.

💡

How this is calculated: Available hours are derived from each asset's weekly capacity multiplied by the number of weeks in your selected date range. Actual operating hours come from telematics. Daily utilisation is actual hours divided by that day's available hours. Set available hours and target utilisation on the Assets page.
