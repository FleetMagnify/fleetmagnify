Emissions Analysis

CO₂ & Greenhouse Gas Reporting

Auditable emissions derived from actual fuel consumption — split between operating and idle activity for NGER-style disclosure.

💡

How this is calculated: Total litres come from your fuel records. Idle fuel is estimated using each asset's idle burn rate multiplied by idle hours from telematics — the same method as Fuel Analyst. Operating fuel is the remainder. CO₂ is converted using emission factors based on the asset's fuel type. Set fuel type and idle burn rate on the Assets page.
