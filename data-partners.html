Integrations

FleetMagnify works with your existing telematics and fuel card providers via automated daily email reports. No API connections. No new hardware. Compatible with any provider that exports structured CSV data.

Telematics

Supported Telematics

eROAD

Truck Telematics

NZ-built telematics platform widely used across NZ truck fleets. Provides daily odometer/mileage reports for cost-per-km calculation.

Navman

Truck & Machinery Telematics

Provides daily mileage and idle reports. FleetMagnify ingests both the Executive Summary Mileage Report and Idle Report to calculate driving vs idle fuel costs.

VisionLink

Machinery Telematics (Caterpillar)

Caterpillar's telematics platform for Cat machinery. Provides operating hours, idle hours, and fuel consumption data for job cost and idle cost analysis.

Komtrax

Machinery Telematics (Komatsu)

Komatsu's telematics platform. Provides daily machine hours and operational data for Komatsu excavators, dozers, and loaders.

CareTrack

Machinery Telematics (Volvo)

Volvo Construction Equipment's telematics system. Provides operating hours and machine data for Volvo machinery fleets.

ConSite

Machinery Telematics (Hitachi)

Hitachi's telematics platform for excavators and construction equipment. Provides machine hours and operational data.

FleetPin

Truck & Light Vehicle Telematics

Popular NZ telematics provider for smaller truck and light vehicle fleets. Provides GPS tracking and mileage data.

Fuel Cards

Supported Fuel Cards

BP

Fuel Cards

BP fuel card transaction exports provide ground-truth fuel purchase data — litres, cost, date, and vehicle reference — used as the foundation for all fill-to-fill cost calculations.

Any Provider

Universal Compatibility

FleetMagnify can ingest fuel transaction data from any provider that exports structured CSV data including date, vehicle reference, litres purchased, and total cost.
