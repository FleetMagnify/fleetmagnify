How It Works

Up and running
quickly.

Step 01  — Connect

Plug in your data sources

Connect your telematics provider and fuel card accounts via our pre-built data integrations. No spreadsheets, no manual uploads. Data begins flowing immediately upon authorisation.

Step 02  — Configure

Set your asset benchmarks

Define your target utilisation rates, idle thresholds, and depreciation parameters per asset class. FleetMagnify uses these to calculate the cost impact specific to your operation.

Step 03  — Analyse

Recover cost, every month

Access live dashboards, schedule automated reports, and identify the highest-cost assets on any job.
